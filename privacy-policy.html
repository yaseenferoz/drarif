Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information submitted through this website is collected, used and protected for appointment and enquiry purposes related to Dr. Arif Raza and NK Hospital, Kalaburagi.

Information We Collect

When you submit an appointment or contact form, we may collect your name, mobile number, email address, consultation type, preferred appointment date and time, and the symptoms or details you choose to share.

How We Use Information

Your information is used to respond to enquiries, schedule appointments, confirm availability, provide clinic communication, and maintain appointment records. We may contact you by phone, email, SMS or WhatsApp based on the details you provide.

Medical Information

Please do not submit emergency medical information through this website. Form submissions are used for appointment coordination and are not a substitute for in-person consultation, diagnosis or emergency care.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with clinic or hospital staff involved in appointment handling, patient communication, care coordination, technical support, or where required by law.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect submitted information. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may contact the clinic to request correction or removal of appointment information where legally and operationally possible. Some records may need to be retained for legitimate medical, administrative or legal reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact NK Hospital, Kalaburagi at +91 91879 66771 or dr.raza@nkhospital.in.
