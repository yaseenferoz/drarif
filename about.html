CONSULTANT SURGICAL GASTROENTEROLOGIST

Focused GI, HPB and Cancer Surgery Care in Kalaburagi

Dr. Arif Raza is a Consultant Gastrointestinal, GI Oncology, HPB and Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon with more than 13 years of surgical experience. He trained in Surgical Gastroenterology at the Asian Institute of Gastroenterology, Hyderabad, and returned to Kalaburagi to make advanced digestive surgery accessible closer to home.

His work spans complex gastrointestinal cancer surgery, liver and pancreatic procedures, colorectal surgery, hernia care, bariatric surgery, endoscopy, colonoscopy and ERCP. Patients are guided with clear explanations, careful diagnosis and treatment plans built around safety, recovery and long-term wellbeing.

CARE PHILOSOPHY

Clear Decisions, Safe Surgery and Compassionate Follow-Up

Digestive conditions can be stressful for patients and families. Dr. Arif Raza's approach is to explain the diagnosis plainly, discuss treatment choices honestly and recommend surgery only when it is the right path for the patient's condition.

From first consultation through recovery, the focus stays on evidence-based care, ethical practice, patient safety and dependable communication.
