ABOUT DR. ARIF RAZA

Advanced Gastrointestinal & GI Cancer Care

Dr. Arif Raza is one of North Karnataka's leading Gastrointestinal and GI Oncology Surgeons with over 13 years of surgical practice. After completing his M.Ch. in Surgical Gastroenterology from the prestigious Asian Institute of Gastroenterology (AIG), Hyderabad, he returned to Kalaburagi with a mission to make advanced digestive and cancer surgery accessible locally.

He has successfully performed more than 5,000 surgeries, including over 700 complex GI Oncology procedures. His expertise includes Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery, GI Cancer Surgery, Liver & Pancreatic Surgery, Colorectal Surgery, Bariatric Surgery, Endoscopy and ERCP, delivering world-class treatment with compassion and precision.
